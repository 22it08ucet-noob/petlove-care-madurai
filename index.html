🩹 First Aid Tips
Quick tips while you wait for the vet
🐶 Dog First Aid
▼
• Keep the dog calm and in a quiet, safe space
• For bleeding — apply gentle pressure with a clean cloth for 5 mins
• Do NOT give human medicines — call the vet immediately
• If unconscious — check breathing, keep airway clear
🐱 Cat First Aid
▼
• Wrap the cat gently in a towel to reduce stress
• If vomiting — do not give food; provide small amounts of water
• Keep in a dark, quiet room to reduce anxiety
🐦 Bird First Aid
▼
• Keep the bird warm — put in a small box with air holes
• Minimize handling — stress can be fatal for birds
🐄 Cow First Aid
▼
• Keep the cow away from other animals
• For bloating — keep the cow moving, call vet immediately
• For wounds — clean with saline, apply antiseptic
🐰 Rabbit First Aid
▼
• Handle gently and minimally — easily stressed
• Keep warm — body temperature drops rapidly during illness
• Never restrict food more than 12 hours — can be fatal
